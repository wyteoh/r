Impact of Food Waste on Sustainable Development

Statistics & Facts

40% of food in the U.S. is wasted annually.
Reducing food waste by just 25% could feed 870 million hungry people.
Food waste contributes to 8-10% of global greenhouse gas emissions.

Environmental Impact

  • Food waste consumes 24% of freshwater resources and 28% of farmland annually

  • It generates 8-10% of global greenhouse gas emissions, contributing significantly to climate change.

  • Decomposing food waste in landfills releases large amounts of methane, a potent greenhouse gas.
